About ITI Focused Equity Fund

ITI Mutual Fund announced the launch of the ITI Focused Equity Fund, an open-ended equity fund scheme investing in a maximum of 30 stocks across market capitalization. The scheme opened for public subscription on May 29, 2023, and will close on June 12, 2023. The scheme re-opens for continuous sale and repurchase on or before June 23, 2023.
